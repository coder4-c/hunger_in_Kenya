From Drought to Prosperity: The Kibet Family's Journey

Turkana County, Northern Kenya December 2024

When the worst drought in decades hit northern Kenya, the Kibet family faced an impossible choice: migrate to cities or watch their children go hungry. Today, they're not just surviving—they're thriving and helping other families do the same.

"Two years ago, we thought we would lose everything. The drought had killed our livestock, and our children were getting sick from hunger. Now look at us—we have enough food for our family, and we're even helping other families learn how to farm in this climate."
- Grace Kibet, Mother of 4

The Impact

  • Family of 6 now food secure year-round
  • 300% increase in crop yields using drought-resistant seeds
  • Grace trained 15 other women in modern farming
  • Children now attend school regularly with improved nutrition
