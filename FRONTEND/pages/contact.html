Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about getting in touch with us

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters are addressed within 4 hours.

Can I visit your office in person?

Yes! We welcome visitors at our Nairobi office. Please call ahead to schedule an appointment so we can ensure the right team member is available to meet with you.

Do you have representatives in other countries?

While our main operations are in Kenya, we work with partner organizations worldwide. Contact us to connect with regional representatives.

How can I volunteer remotely?

We offer many remote volunteer opportunities. Email our volunteer coordinator at volunteers@hungerinkenya.org or fill out our volunteer application form.

What's the best way to donate?

Visit our donation page for multiple giving options including online donations, mobile money, and bank transfers. Contact donations@hungerinkenya.org for assistance.

How can my organization partner with you?

We welcome partnerships with corporations, NGOs, and institutions. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@hungerinkenya.org to explore collaboration opportunities.
